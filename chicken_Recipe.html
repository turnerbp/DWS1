Aimee's Quick Chicken

This is recipe I use for a quick Chicken Dinner.'"

Recipe from All Recipes - http://allrecipes.com

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 4 ounces Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 cup teriyaki sauce
  • 1/4 cup bacon bits
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. 1.Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. 2.Place chicken in a 9x13 inch baking dish. Slather mustard evenly over chicken, then pour teriyaki sauce evenly over all. Sprinkle with bacon bits, then cover with cheese.
  3. 3.Bake at 400 degrees F (200 degrees C) for 30 minutes.
